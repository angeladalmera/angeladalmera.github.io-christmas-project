To my friends,

Thank you for being my friends and accepting me for who I am. Thank you for giving me the love I never thought I would receive from other people. We’ve only been together for one year but it already feels like we’ve known each other for so long. Thank you because I found a family in you. I’m very grateful to have met each one of you. I hope that we continue to be strong and be together despite all the challenges that we will face. I hope that each of you gets the happiness that we all deserve. I love you guys and I will never forget meeting you in my life.
