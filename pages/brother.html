Dearest Alfonso,

I just want to say that I am so grateful for having you as my brother. Your existence in this world brought so much joy not only to me, but also our family. Thank you for cheering me up whenever I feel so down. I’m really sorry for all the wrong doings I have done to you and thank you for still being good to me despite that. I hope that you will always do what is right even if it is sometimes difficult to do so. Always be thankful to the people who have helped you and don’t forget to also help other people. Remember that I am always here for you. I may not be the best sister but I will do everything I can to give you everything you need. I love you always.
